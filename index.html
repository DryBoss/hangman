Hangman

Classic

Experience the timeless classic of Hangman.

Objective:
  • Guess the randomly selected word within 6 attempts.

Word Challenge

Challenge a friend to a head-to-head Hangman showdown.

Objective:
  • One player writes a word and submits it.
  • The other player must guess the word within 6 attempts.

Duel

Engage in a fast-paced duel with a friend to see who can guess the word first.

Objective:
  • Guess the word before your opponent.
  • If your guessed letter is correct, continue guessing until you finish the word.
  • If incorrect, it's your opponent's turn.
  • The game alternates until one player successfully guesses the word.

Tournament Duel

Compete in a tournament-style duel with friends to guess a word submitted by another player.

Objective:
  • One player selects a word for the other two to guess.
  • The two players take turns guessing letters.
  • Each incorrect guess switches turns between the two players.
  • The game ends when one player correctly guesses the word.
